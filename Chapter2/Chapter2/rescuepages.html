Welcome to Wild Rescues. Our missionn is to rescue, rehabilitate, and release wildlife back into their natural environment. We are a nonprofit orginization dedicated to helping injured wildlife.

Our staff provides around-the-clock care for wildlife in need. We also provide long-term care for wildlife that cannot be released back into their natural environement.
